FoodHub Demand Analysis

Posted on 2025-09-2025

Tags: Python, Pandas, Seaborn, Matplotlib, Exploratory Data Analysis, Customer Analytics

Problem:

FoodHub, a fictional food delivery platform, sought to better understand customer ordering behavior and operational inefficiencies. The objective was to explore order data to uncover trends, performance issues, and opportunities for strategic improvements in customer satisfaction and logistics.

Approach:

  • Cleaned and explored order-level data using Pandas
  • Performed Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to identify trends in:
    • Order volume over time
    • Cuisine popularity by region
    • Customer ratings and satisfaction
  • Visualized findings using Seaborn and Matplotlib to illustrate key business patterns
  • Analyzed peak demand days and hours, along with delivery performance metrics

Outcome:

  • Identified that weekends drive significantly higher order volumes, suggesting operational planning opportunities
  • Uncovered American and Italian cuisines as top performers — insights for promotional targeting
  • Observed a negative correlation between delivery times and customer satisfaction, indicating a need for better route optimization and delivery staffing
  • Generated business recommendations to:
    • Enhance delivery logistics during peak hours
    • Boost marketing on high-performing cuisines
    • Improve delivery speed to raise customer ratings
