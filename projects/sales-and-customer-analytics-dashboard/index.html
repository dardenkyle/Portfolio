Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard

Posted on 2025-09-2025

Tags: Power BI, SQL, Data Cleaning, Business Intelligence, KPI Reporting

Problem:

The client needed a clear understanding of how different product categories, customer segments, and regions were contributing to overall revenue and profitability. Existing reporting was siloed and inconsistent, limiting strategic decisions.

Approach:

  • Cleaned and transformed the Superstore dataset using Power Query and SQL
  • Designed a relational data model to support flexible analysis
  • Developed calculated fields and DAX measures for KPIs like:
    • Profit margin
    • Sales by category/segment
    • Regional performance and trendlines
  • Created a responsive, multi-page Power BI dashboard with:
    • Drill-down functionality
    • Dynamic filters by region, segment, and date
    • Visualizations for top products, profit breakdown, and category contribution

Outcome:

  • Delivered a professional-grade dashboard that allows users to:
    • Monitor KPIs in real-time
    • Identify underperforming regions or categories
    • Drill into customer and product-level data to inform sales strategy
  • Demonstrated the value of self-serve BI in reducing reporting overhead and improving decision-making agility
